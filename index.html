Michael J. Bernstein

Researcher · Facilitator · Leader

Michael Bernstein is a researcher, facilitator, and leader working at the boundaries of science, policy, and practice. For 15 years, he has helped diverse teams and institutions stretch toward social and ecological goals.

He brings rigorous analytical thinking and genuine care for people to some of the most consequential challenges of our time — from democratic governance to innovation system design for broad public benefit.

He currently serves as a Senior Scientist and Research Field Leader at the Austrian Institute of Technology.
